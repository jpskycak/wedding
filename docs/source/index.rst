Welcome to Sanjana & Justin's wedding site!
===================================

To keep the URL reasonably short, we used the portmanteau ``SanJustin = Sanjana + Justin``. (*Sanju* is a diminutive of Sanjana.)

.. and offers a *simple* and *intuitive* API.
